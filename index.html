Fengze 'Liam' Li

I'm a Ph.D Candidate at University of Liverpool, advised by Prof. Jieming Ma, Dr. Zhongbei Tian, and Dr. Hai-Ning Liang. My research focuses on computer vision tasks and their applications under extreme and uncertain environments, including detection of specular objects in 2D communities and 3D reconstruction of indeterminate real worlds.

Before starting Ph.D, I had the honor to work with Prof. Jie Zhang at University of Southampton. I've also interned at DJI, Deloitte, and worked as a research assistant at JITRI with Dr. Ji Ge. Currently, I work as the CTO of a startup focusing on 3D tech.
